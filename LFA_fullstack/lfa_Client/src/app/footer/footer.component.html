Privacy Policy for NPC Archive

Introduction:

Welcome to the NPC Archive! Our website is dedicated to providing Dungeon Masters and players with a convenient archive of Non-Player Characters (NPCs) for use in their campaigns. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal information you may provide.

Information We Collect:

1. User-Provided Information:
When you voluntarily submit NPC creations or other content to our website, we collect the information you provide, including NPC details, names, and any other information you choose to share.

2. Automatically Collected Information:
Like many websites, we may collect certain information automatically, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system. This information helps us enhance the user experience and troubleshoot any technical issues.

How We Use Your Information:

1. NPC Archive:
User-provided NPC information is used to populate our archive, making it accessible to the DND community. Contributions are shared anonymously unless the user provides a name or username for attribution.

2. Improvement of Services:
Automatically collected information is used for analytical purposes to improve our website's functionality, performance, and user interface.

3. Communication:
If you choose to contact us through the provided contact form or email, we may use your email address to respond to your inquiries, comments, or feedback.

Data Security:

We take appropriate measures to protect the information submitted to us, both during transmission and once received. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure.

Cookies:

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You have the option to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. Please note that declining cookies may limit your access to certain features of the site.

Third-Party Links:

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage users to read the privacy policies of any linked websites.

Updates to Privacy Policy:

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The effective date at the top of the policy will indicate when the latest changes were made. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policy regularly.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@npcarchive.com.

Thank you for using NPC Archive! May your campaigns be filled with exciting characters and epic adventures.
